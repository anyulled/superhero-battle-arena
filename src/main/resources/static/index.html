Competition Rules

Teams

Each team must consist of at least 2 distinct superheroes. Choose wisely to balance Strength, Speed, and Durability. A balanced composition is key to victory.

Combat

Battles are turn-based. Priority goes to the faster hero. Attack damage is calculated based on Strength vs. Defense, with elemental tag modifiers applying crucial multipliers.

Winning

A team wins when all opposing heroes are Knocked Out (KO). If the turn limit (50) is reached, the match ends in a Draw. Points are awarded for wins and survival.
